TheShwarma Represents:

Text Shortener Algorithm

  • Common words are replaced with number according to rarity
  • Numbers sorrunded with quatation marks
  • Unknown words are left as is
  • File extension: .STF (Short Text File)

For example:
Sentence I won the game 10 times, that was fun becomes 18 won 1 484 "10" times, 10 12 586.
I is the 18th most common word and therefore becomes 18. Won is not common an thus is left as it is.
The 10 is surrounded with quotation marks to not make it confused as a code.
