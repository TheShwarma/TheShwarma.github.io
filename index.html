SendME

 Free messaging app

Why choose SendME?

  • Since I (only one developer) don't work for a company, I have no reason to steal your data and sell it like other messaging apps do. 
  • It doesn't run from a web browser, so if you have malware plug-ins on your browser for some reason, they won't be able to get your data.
  • Strong AES encryption (coming soon, assets placed).
  • Lightweight, app weighs less then 25 Mega-bytes, with useally small internet traffic. 
  • Free. Unless I do a payed version, which in this case the free version will have about the same features and will be updated as well.
  • Passwords encrypted using BLAKE-2, even I don't know your passwords once it is encrypted.
  • Easy to use.
  • Terminal based.
